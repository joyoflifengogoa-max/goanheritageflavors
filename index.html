How to Use

For Vindaloo: Marinate 500g pork with 2 tbsp masalo, vinegar, garlic. Slow cook till tender.

For Cafreal: Grind 2 tbsp masalo with coriander, green chili. Marinate chicken. Pan fry.

For Xacuti: Roast 2 tbsp masalo with coconut. Add to chicken/mutton curry.
