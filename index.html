Goan Heritage Flavors

450 Years of Indo-Portuguese Recipes | Preserved by Goan Grandmothers
“Fiery red. Tangy and spicy equals Vindaloo born.”

But these flavors are disappearing.

Joy of Life NGO Goa partners with Senior Citizen Self Help Groups to preserve authentic recipes from our ancestors.

Every jar is hand-ground by Goan grandmothers who need income, who have the talent, who carry 450 years in their hands.

This is not charity. This is heritage.
When you buy, a grandmother earns. A recipe lives.
